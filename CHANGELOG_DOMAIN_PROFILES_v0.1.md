# Changelog — Domain Profiles v0.1

## Legal Domain Profile v0.1

* Revised the legal profile from common-law leaning terminology into a jurisdiction-neutral legal translation profile.
* Added a jurisdiction-neutral interpretation section.
* Added `jurisdictional\\\\\\\_adaptation\\\\\\\_note.md` for local legal-system translation.
* Expanded legal-domain translation terms to include adjudicative, administrative, regulatory, institutional, and rights-affecting contexts.

* Added expiry, review, withdrawal, and renewal concepts to operational governance questions.
* Updated the legal adoption checklist, risk-benefit matrix, governance mapping, court/tribunal adoption guide, and legal LBA example for jurisdiction-neutral adoption.
